# MrShinyWhiteTiger
## Full title: *The Incredible Mr. Shiny in: The White Tiger Hunt*

Take on the role of the world's sharpest bounty hunter, **Mr. Shiny**, as he races against the clock to scale the White Tiger smuggling ring's **fortified tower** and take down the **sinister Director Baek**. Use his signature **grappling hook** to zip around the tower and outmaneuver your enemies, and earn **style points** for taking them out in the flashiest way possible! Can you take out the White Tiger **before time runs out?**

---

*The Incredible Mr. Shiny in: The White Tiger Hunt* is an arcade-style brawler game by Alec Pike. **Coming May 16th!**
